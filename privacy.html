Privacy

Last updated: July 2026

SPLASH! is currently in pre-launch. This page explains what we collect on this waitlist page today, and what will change once the app itself launches.

What we collect right now

Just your email address, if you join the waitlist. That's it — this page doesn't use tracking cookies beyond basic, anonymized analytics to see how many people visit.

What we do with it

We'll use your email for exactly one thing: telling you when SPLASH! is ready to try. We won't sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it for anything else.

When the app launches

Once SPLASH! is a real, installable app, this policy will be replaced with a full privacy policy covering what the app itself collects (e.g., study session data, streaks, and any account information), consistent with Google Play's Data Safety requirements.
